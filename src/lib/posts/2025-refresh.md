Years ago, I built a portfolio site that I thought was pretty neat.

The whole layout was built around the concept of hovering over my avatar, and having it morph into different themes.
Cool right? Well, problem is over the years, my main "themes" and interests have changed, and I'd love to be able to update my site without needing to re-implement a new svg animation that rotates into my big head.

Meet the new johalloran.dev! It's a bit more minimalistic, but I think it's a bit more "me" these days.

It will be a bit of a work-in-progress for a while, but I'm excited to have a fresh start for the new year.
