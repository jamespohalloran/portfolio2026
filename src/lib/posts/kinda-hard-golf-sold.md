Big news: [Kinda Hard Golf](https://kindahardgolf.com) has new owners.

Last month I shipped the 400th daily level, which was hard to believe. I've loved building this game and watching it grow, but it's time for me to pass the torch and focus on something new. The opportunity presented itself to sell the game to a great group of daily-game veterans, and the timing just felt right.

In the early days, I'd hand-place every tile, every day (I didn't even having auto-tiling until day 100). I'd wake up every morning, check my phone in fear of seeing a "This level is busted" message. Over the last year that pipeline got smoother and smoother, and it's now in a great place for the next stewards to take the reins.

This one was tough to let go of. It's the first project I've built on my own that grew to this scale. But like the game itself, tomorrow's a whole new level.

## Thank you 💚

To everyone who played, shared a score, told a friend, or sent the link to their favourite streamer: thank you. To everyone who streamed it, I watched more of it than I'd care to admit. Massive shoutout to all the streamers who played it on stream. (Northernlion and Bodegarrat in particular!).
