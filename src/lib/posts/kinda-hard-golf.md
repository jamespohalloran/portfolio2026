A few times a year, I will think up an idea that glues me to the keyboard for a few weeks. This is one of those times.

Introducing...

![Kinda Hard Golf](https://kindahardgolf.com/hero.jpg)

Play it Free 🎮: https://kindahardgolf.com

How did I come up with the name? Well...when I showed people the game, they would say "It's fun! But kinda hard".

The game was built in PixiJS over a few weeks. I've had a great time making it and will continue tweaking, and looking for a home for it in the future.
Hope you like it!
